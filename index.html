Average Loan Amount vs Average Annual Income

  • With time, loans were issued to people with average annual income between 50k and 100k.
  • With time, average loan amounts were between 10k and 25k.
  • LendUp tends to develop a stable loan issuing pattern.

    • Main Grade for Loans

  • Grade B loan was issued most before 2012, while the trend declined thereafter.
  • The number of grade A loan varies a lot but presents an increasing tendency since 2013.
  • Grade F and grade G loans were always issued least with a stable decreasing trend.
